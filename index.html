Our Story

Passion for Coffee,
Purpose for Rwanda

Bender Exports Ltd was founded in 2017 by Mr. Horanimpundu Jean Paul and Ms. Uwamariya Odette — driven by a conviction that Rwanda's extraordinary coffee deserved a world stage, and that farmer's who grow it deserved to directly share in its value.

Through modern washing infrastructure, secure warehousing, and hands-on farmer training, we ensure quality is protected at every step — from cherry delivery to international shipment.

From Farm to Export

How We
Build Quality

Every Bender Exports coffee lot follows a meticulous five-step journey. From the moment a farmer delivers ripe cherries to the day green beans leave Rwanda, quality is never left to chance.

01
Cherry Harvest
Hand-picked ripe red cherries delivered by partner farmers
02
Wet Processing
Pulping, controlled fermentation, and thorough washing
03
Sun Drying
Raised African beds under equatorial sun, turned regularly
04
Milling & Grading
Hulling, screen sorting, density grading, secure warehousing
05
QC & Export
Cupping, final quality checks, international shipment
